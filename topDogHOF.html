2018 Top Dog is Ed Devin

We have a two-time Top Dog in our midst. His name is Ed Devin. The Captain. Eddie parlayed Depot tips into a second Top Dog title, tallying nearly $3,000 in donations to take the top spot for our 15th annual plunge.

2017 Top Dog is Dan McDonagh

Upon completing the hat trick in Topdogdom, in 2014, Dan McDonagh became the one and only Triple Top Dog Tug. Now, achieving this highest of Jolly Jump honors a 4th time, he is clearly the GOAT (Greatest of All Time) in fundraising. His tally of $3,450 is also the most any single jumper has raised in the entire history of The Jump.

2016 Top Dog is JT Nickley

JT raised more than $3,000 this year, shattering all previous records for the top Jolly Jump fundraiser. We also had 11 Jolly Jumpers clear $1,000 in personal fundraising efforts, in 2016. And with 50 jumpers strong this year, Deb & Jackie's Jolly Jump raised an astounding, unprecedented $28,943 ($27,128 online and another $1,815 offline)!

2015 Top Dog is Eddie Devin, $1,874!

In our 12 years taking the plunge, 2015 may have been our closest race to the Top Dog yet. Triple Top Dog Dan "Tugboat" McDonagh, held the lead for much of the race, but Sir Eddie Devin pulled ahead in the 11th hour to take down the crown and claim his space on the coveted plaque. 2015 was also our most successful year to date. We raised more than $12,500 in total, with four of the top 5 earners clearing a $1,000!

2014 TOP DOG: Dan McDonagh, $1,657.50

Dan "Tugboat" McDonagh is the only Jolly Jumper to receive this honor three times, which makes him Triple Top Dog. In 2015, he was on pace to take the honor a fourth time, but 2015 Top Dog Eddie Devin pulled ahead with a whopping $1,874, narrowly edging out the reigning Top Dog Tug. It should be noted here, that after several late donation arrivals (and after officially crowning the 2015 Top Dog), Tug did tally a total of $1,885.
